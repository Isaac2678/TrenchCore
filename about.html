Our Story

Colorado Ground,
Colorado Crew

TrenchCore was founded in 2008 by Marcus Webb, a Colorado native with over a decade of commercial excavation experience across the Front Range. After years working for larger contractors, Marcus built TrenchCore around a straightforward idea: a crew small enough to care and skilled enough to compete with anyone.

We started with a single excavator and a focus on residential irrigation. Today, TrenchCore runs six service lines — trenching, irrigation, grading, drainage, utility installation, and hardscaping — with a team of 12 certified operators working across the state.

Licensed, insured, and bonded in Colorado. Every job is overseen by a project manager from first call to final cleanup. No subcontracting to strangers — you get TrenchCore people and TrenchCore accountability.
